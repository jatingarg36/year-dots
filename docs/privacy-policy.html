Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

Year Progress Dots ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the App handles information.

Data We Collect

Year Progress Dots does not collect any personal data. We do not collect your name, email, location, or any other personally identifiable information.

Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores your preferences locally on your device only. This includes:

  • Dot size, spacing, and grid layout
  • Color choices for completed days, today, and future days
  • Display options (e.g., show today highlight, start week on Sunday)

This data never leaves your device. It is stored using your device's built-in storage (AsyncStorage on mobile, similar mechanisms on web) and is used only to provide your customized experience.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking. We do not share any data with third parties.

Children

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children. It is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the Year-Dots GitHub repository.
